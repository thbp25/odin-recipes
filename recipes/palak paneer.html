Palak Paneer

Description

Palak Paneer (lit.transl. Spinach Cottage Cheese) is an Indian dish consisting of paneer (cottage cheese) in a thick curried paste made from puréed spinach. It is typically served alongside roti (Indian flatbread) or boiled rice.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained/li>
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger root
  • 2 cups cubed paneer

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in hot oil until slightly tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, red pepper flakes, curry powder, cumin, and salt; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Mix spinach, tomatoes, water, and ginger into onion mixture; simmer for 20 minutes. Remove from heat and cool slightly, about 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer spinach mixture to a blender and blend until smooth. Set aside.
  5. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in the same skillet over medium heat; cook and stir paneer in hot oil until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in puréed spinach mixture and cook until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
  7. Your delicious Palak Paneer is ready. Enjoy!
